## Context
Client is deploying a Ubiquiti UniFi WiFi network. OTMA is responsible for the full scope: assessing coverage needs, planning AP placement, procuring and installing equipment, and handing off a working network.

## Objective
Deliver a fully operational UniFi WiFi network — properly sized, installed, and configured — so the client has stable coverage across their space with no further setup needed on their end.

## Scope
**In scope:**
- Site survey and coverage assessment
- AP count and placement recommendation
- Equipment list finalization (controller, switches)
- Equipment procurement coordination
- Physical installation of APs
- Network configuration and commissioning (SSIDs, VLANs, security)
- Client handoff and basic documentation

**Out of scope:**
- ISP / modem setup (unless confirmed)
- Structured cabling / patch panels (unless confirmed)
- Ongoing managed IT support (unless contracted separately)
