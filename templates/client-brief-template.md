# Client Brief Template

## 1. Business Overview
- What does your business do?
- Who are your customers?
- How long have you been operating?
- What is the main product or service you want the website to focus on?

## 2. Goals
- What is the primary goal of this website? (e.g. generate leads, sell online, establish credibility)
- What does a successful outcome look like for you?
- Is there a launch deadline?

## 3. Target Audience
- Who is your ideal customer? (age, profession, location, lifestyle)
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- What makes them choose you over a competitor?

## 4. Positioning
- How do you want to be perceived? (premium, accessible, technical, friendly, etc.)
- What makes you different from competitors?
- What do customers usually say about you after working with you?

## 5. Pages & Content
- What pages do you need? (e.g. Home, About, Services, Contact)
- Do you have existing copy or should it be written from scratch?
- Do you have a logo and brand guidelines?
- Do you have photos or do we need to source them?

## 6. Visual Direction
- Share 3–5 websites you like and what you like about them.
- Any colors, fonts, or styles to use or avoid?
- Any existing brand assets (logo, colors, typography)?

## 7. Technical
- Do you have a domain? If so, where is it registered?
- Do you need a blog or CMS?
- Do you need a contact form or booking system?
- Any integrations needed? (e.g. Mailchimp, Calendly, WhatsApp)

## 8. Budget & Timeline
- What is your budget range?
- When do you need the site live?
- Are there any important dates to work around?

## 9. Other Notes
- Anything else we should know before starting?
