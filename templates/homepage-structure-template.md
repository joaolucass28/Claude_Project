# Homepage Structure Template

Default section order for client homepages. Adjust based on business type and goal.

---

## 1. Nav
- Logo
- Main links (3–5 max)
- Primary CTA button (sticky on scroll)

## 2. Hero
- Headline: clear value proposition in one sentence
- Subheadline: who it's for and what they get
- Primary CTA button
- Supporting visual (photo, video, or illustration)

## 3. Social Proof Bar
- Logos of clients, partners, or press mentions
- Or a single strong stat (e.g. "50+ clients served")

## 4. Problem / Context
- Name the pain or situation the customer is in
- Create relevance before presenting the solution

## 5. Solution / Offer
- What you do and how it works
- 3-step process or feature highlights
- Focus on outcomes, not features

## 6. Benefits
- 3–6 key benefits with icons or short descriptions
- Frame from the customer's perspective

## 7. Proof
- Testimonials (real names, photos if possible)
- Case studies or before/after results
- Numbers or certifications if relevant

## 8. About / Trust
- Brief intro to the person or team behind the business
- Why you do this work
- Builds human connection before the final CTA

## 9. FAQ
- 4–6 common objections or questions
- Keep answers short and direct

## 10. Final CTA
- Restate the core offer
- Strong action-oriented CTA button
- Optional: low-commitment secondary option (e.g. "or book a free call")

## 11. Footer
- Logo
- Nav links
- Contact info
- Social links
- Legal (privacy policy, terms)

---

**Notes:**
- Sections 3 and 8 can be removed for simpler projects.
- For service businesses, lead with the outcome in the hero, not the process.
- Always end with a clear next step.
